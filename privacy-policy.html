Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Classic Tyres ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number when you contact us or place an order.
  • Payment information when processing transactions (handled securely).
  • Browsing data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for purposes such as:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Improving our website and services.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties such as payment processors, delivery companies, and IT service providers, only where necessary to provide our services.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR for EU residents), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
