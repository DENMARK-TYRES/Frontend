Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve performance, to remember preferences, and to provide analytics and advertising features.

2) Who sets cookies?

Cookies may be set by us (“first-party cookies”) or by third parties (“third-party cookies”) that provide services on our site (e.g., analytics or advertising partners).

We are the controller for first-party cookies and for the selection of third-party tools used on this site. Some third parties act as independent controllers for the data they collect via their cookies. See their privacy notices for details.

3) Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary (always active): Required for core site functionality such as page navigation, security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Functional: Remember choices (e.g., language, region) to provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use the website (e.g., pages viewed, time on site) so we can improve performance.
  • Advertising/Marketing: Used to deliver ads more relevant to you, measure campaign effectiveness, and limit ad frequency.

4) Legal basis & consent

We only place non-essential cookies (Functional, Analytics, Advertising) with your consent. You can give or withdraw consent at any time via the “Cookie Settings” link in our footer or banner. Strictly necessary cookies are used based on our legitimate interest in providing a secure, functioning website.
