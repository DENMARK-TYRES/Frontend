Frequently Asked Questions

What types of tyres do you offer?

We specialize in classic, vintage, and specialty tyres, as well as rims that fit a wide variety of vehicles. Whether you need tyres for restoration projects, racing, or everyday driving, we have you covered.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship tyres and rims across Europe and internationally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location.

How can I check if a product is in stock?

Our webshop is updated in real-time. If you see a product listed, it’s available for order. For rare or limited items, feel free to contact us for confirmation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards and bank transfers options.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, unused products can be returned or exchanged within 14 days of delivery.
